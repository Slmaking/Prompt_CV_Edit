# Interview Preparation Prompts & Example Responses

This repository provides structured prompts and examples to help prepare for job interviews. It includes guidance on behavioral questions, technical questions, scenario-based questions, personal storytelling, and industry research.

---

# 1. Behavioral Interview Questions

**Prompt**

Act as an expert in Human Resources specializing in crafting and evaluating interview questions. Provide a comprehensive list of behavioral interview questions tailored for the **[job title]**.

The questions should:

* Reveal insights about the candidate’s past work experiences, behaviors, and decision-making.
* Focus on competencies such as:

  * Teamwork
  * Problem-solving
  * Leadership
  * Communication
  * Adaptability
* Encourage candidates to share **specific examples from past experiences**.

Additionally, include guidance on **what ideal responses should contain**, helping interviewers evaluate candidate potential and cultural fit.

---

# 2. Technical Interview Questions

**Prompt**

Act as an HR expert assistant and compile a list of **technical interview questions** for the **[job title]**.

The questions should:

* Assess the candidate’s **technical expertise and depth of knowledge**
* Evaluate **problem-solving skills**
* Include **scenario-based technical questions**
* Test **adaptability and learning agility with new technologies**

The goal is to identify candidates who:

* Meet technical requirements
* Demonstrate analytical thinking
* Align with the company’s values and culture.

---

# 3. Scenario-Based Interview Questions

**Prompt**

Act as an HR expert assistant tasked with crafting **scenario-based questions** for the **[job title]**.

These questions should:

* Evaluate **problem-solving abilities**
* Assess **strategic thinking**
* Reflect **real-world situations** related to the role
* Encourage candidates to explain **how they would handle challenges**

Questions should be **open-ended** to generate deeper insights into the candidate’s mindset and approach.

---

# 4. Answering “Tell Me About Yourself”

**Prompt**

Act as an interview expert and craft a compelling answer to the **“Tell Me About Yourself”** question based on the candidate’s background.

The response should:

* Highlight **relevant experience and achievements**
* Tell a **clear career story**
* Connect past experience with the **current role**
* Demonstrate **motivation and future potential**

The answer should balance **professional accomplishments with personal insights**, leaving a strong first impression.

---

# 5. Discussing Strengths and Weaknesses

**Prompt**

Create a guide on how to effectively discuss **strengths and weaknesses** during an interview.

The guide should include:

### Strengths

* Align strengths with **job requirements**
* Provide **real examples**
* Demonstrate measurable impact

### Weaknesses

* Show **self-awareness**
* Explain **how you are improving**
* Frame weaknesses as **growth opportunities**

The goal is to balance **confidence and humility** while leaving a positive impression.

---

# 6. Discussing Career Goals

**Prompt**

Craft a strategic response describing **career goals** during a job interview.

The response should:

* Present **short-term and long-term goals**
* Align with the **company’s mission**

# Example Experience (CAR Method)

To answer behavioral questions effectively, you can use the **CAR method**:

* **Context** – Describe the situation and your role
* **Action** – Explain what you did
* **Result** – Show the measurable outcome

# Example CAR Story

## Project: Smartphone Data Reorientation Algorithm

### Context

At Intact, I worked on a project aligning **smartphone sensor data with vehicle telemetry data** to improve accuracy in driving behavior analysis. I led the technical work while collaborating with an external data provider.

### Action

I implemented **A/B testing and statistical validation** to evaluate the data quality. To handle missing data more effectively, I introduced **second-degree interpolation using NumPy in Python**, replacing the previous Azure query approach.

Through further analysis, I discovered issues with the provider’s data reorientation process and developed an **internal solution**.

### Result

The new approach increased the **correlation between data features by 35%**, significantly improving data reliability. This led to a **policy change within the company** for handling this dataset.
